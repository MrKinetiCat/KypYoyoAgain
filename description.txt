Kypsie's Grand Yoyo Extravaganza reminagines what it means to be a "yoyo master."
With plans for over twenty new and exciting yoyos as well as at least half a dozen yoyo related accessories, this mod will be your end all be all for everything yoyo related.

Contains the following:
	- One crafting station
	- One throwable item
	- One sentry weapon
	- Sixteen yoyos
	- Five accessories

Hopefully this mod will enrich the yoyo experience for all. I hope you enjoy <3
